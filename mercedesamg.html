Mercedes-AMG

The Anatomy of Performance

The Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team is one of the most successful constructors in Formula 1 history, renowned for its technical innovation and relentless pursuit of performance. While the "Silver Arrows" first dominated the sport in the mid-1950s with racing legend Juan Manuel Fangio, the modern iteration of the team was born when Mercedes returned to the grid full-time in 2010.

Led by Team Principal Toto Wolff and powered by the driving brilliance of Lewis Hamilton, the team triggered an unprecedented era of modern dominance at the start of the V6 turbo-hybrid era in 2014, capturing a historic eight consecutive Constructors' Championships from 2014 to 2021 and securing nine total Drivers' Titles.
