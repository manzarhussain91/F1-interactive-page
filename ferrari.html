Scrudria Ferrari

The Legacy of the Prancing Horse

Scuderia Ferrari is the racing division of the luxury Italian auto manufacturer Ferrari and represents the oldest and most successful team in Formula One history. Since its inception in 1929 by Enzo Ferrari, the team has been defined by its pursuit of engineering excellence and its iconic "Prancing Horse" emblem.

Known for its deep cultural impact and passionate global fanbase, known as the Tifosi, Scuderia Ferrari remains the only team to have competed in every season of the Formula One World Championship. Today, the team continues to balance a rich heritage of innovation.
