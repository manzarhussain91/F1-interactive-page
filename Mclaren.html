Mclaren

McLaren F1: Racing Heritage Reimagined

McLaren is one of Formula 1’s most storied constructors, celebrated for its pioneering spirit, rich racing pedigree, and iconic papaya livery. Founded in 1963 by driver and engineer Bruce McLaren, the British outfit rapidly grew from a scrappy garage operation into an F1 powerhouse.

Over the decades, legendary driver pairings like Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost, alongside champions Niki Lauda, Mika Häkkinen, and Lewis Hamilton, spearheaded golden eras of championship dominance. Guided today by CEO Zak Brown and Team Principal Andrea Stella—and led on track by Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri—McLaren has mounted a triumphant return to the front of the grid, restoring its championship-winning legacy.
