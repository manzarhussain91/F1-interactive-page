The Pinnacle of Motorsport

Formula One is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars. Inaugurated in 1950, it has grown into a global phenomenon combining cutting-edge engineering, extreme speeds, and world-class driver skill. The championship consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix, held worldwide on purpose-built circuits and closed public roads.
